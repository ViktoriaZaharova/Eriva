About flight school Eriva

ERIVA Flight School is a training organization approved by the Civil Aviation Agency of the Republic of Latvia (CAA), which provides training for private pilots and professional pilots.

We started our activity in 2004, having trained our first students as a helicopter pilot. In 2007 we continued to work as an ERIVA flight school. Moreover, the teaching experience of our instructors is much greater.
15

years

of training experience

ERIVA - the first flight school in Latvia, offering its students ATP theoretical training course, as well as flight programmes for professional pilots. As our competitive advantage, we consider the high quality of education that we achieve by making fairly high demands on our students. Our goal is to prepare high-level professional and private pilots.

Compliance with high safety standards is the foundation of our business.

Currently, our students are not only the pilots of leading European and world airlines, but also successfully develop their careers in business aviation. In addition, we are proud that the pilots of the State Border Guard of the Republic of Latvia also study with us.
