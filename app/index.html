About flight school Eriva

ERIVA Flight School is a training organization approved by the Civil Aviation Agency of the Republic of Latvia (CAA), which provides training for private pilots and professional pilots.

We started our activity in 2004, having trained our first students as a helicopter pilot. In 2007 we continued to work as an ERIVA flight school. Moreover, the teaching experience of our instructors is much greater.

Why us?

01

Modular Training

01

Modular-type training gives you the opportunity to learn specific programmes separately. That means you are the leader of your own time. If you want - just get a private pilot license. If you are excited - learn more and become professional comercpilot! Also the financial expenses will be gradual, for each model separately

02

Proffesional instructors

02

All our instructors are experienced professionals in the aviation industry who provide support not only in theoretical knowledge but also in their practical experience. They are invaluable advisors for our students at all stages of training.

03

15 years of training experience

03

First pilots were trained already in 2004. Since then, we have expanded the number of training courses and received the status of approved training organization in the European Aviation Safety Agency (EASA). Currently, our students operate aircraft in almost all of Europe's leading airlines
